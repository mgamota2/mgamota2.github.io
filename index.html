About Me

Welcome! My name is Michael Gamota and I am a senior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign majoring in Electrical Engineering with minors in Computer Science and Business Administration.

I have 3 summers of internship experience. I spent my first two summers of college interning at a research and development institute called NextFlex which has a mission of advancing flexible hybrid electronics (FHE) enabled products for industries including: healthcare, aerospace, defense, consumer electronics, automotive, transportation, and infrastructure. I spent my most recent summer as an Application Engineering intern at Texas Instruments, the largest analog semiconductor company in the world.

At school, I am balancing my academics with practicum, solving real world problems in collaborative environments, ranging from student engineering organizations to undergraduate research. This gives me a unique perspective, allowing me to appreciate the electronics ecosystem, from various places on the R&D spectrum to industry product development and commercialization.

The purpose of this site is to document my experiences as a student, engineer, and developer of innovative solutions.
